SIR

Unofficial Android app for SModcast Internet Radio.

Fan-made · Not affiliated with SModcast

↓ Download APK (Nightly) GitHub

Features

One tap to play

Tap anywhere on screen to start or stop the stream. No clutter.

Sleep timer

Set a timer from 15 minutes to 2 hours and drift off.

Equalizer

Normal, Bass Boost, Vocal, and Treble presets built in.

Chromecast

Cast to any Chromecast-enabled device on your network.

Background playback

Persistent media notification with full playback controls.

Auto-reconnect

Exponential backoff retry after network interruptions.

Wear OS

Standalone companion app for your watch.

Android Auto

Hands-free media controls while driving.

Home widget

Glance widget with Material 3 theming for your home screen.

Install

SIR is currently available as a sideload APK while the Play Store listing is being set up. Download the latest nightly build from GitHub, enable "Install from unknown sources" on your device, and install. SIR is an unofficial, fan-made app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by SModcast or its creators.
